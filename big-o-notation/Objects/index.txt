Unordered, key-value pairs
Great when you don't need order
Great when you do need fast access, insertion, and removal

Big O of basic Object interaction
Insertion - O(1)
Removal - O(1)
Searching - O(n)
Access - O(1)

Big O of Object methods
Object.keys - O(n)
Object.values - O(n)
Object.entries - O(n)
Object.prototype.hasOwnProperty - O(1)
